Places Covered in Ranchi City Tour

Tagore Hill (Morabadi)

Named after Rabindranath Tagore who visited often. 300 ft high hillock with Tagore's meditation house at top. Panoramic city views. Rock climbing popular. Morning visit recommended. Entry free. 15-20 minutes climb. Beautiful sunrise spot.

Rock Garden (Kanke Road)

Beautiful landscaped garden with rock sculptures, waterfalls, artificial lake. Well-maintained paths, boating available. Musical fountain (evening). Entry ₹20. Photography allowed. Popular picnic spot for families. Open 9 AM to 6 PM.

Ranchi Lake (Ranchi Jheel)

Artificial lake in city center with island temple. Boating available (₹50-100). Evening walking track popular. Food stalls around lake. Ducks and swans add charm. Best during sunset. Peaceful environment in busy city.

Pahari Mandir

Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva on hilltop. 468 steps to climb (or drive up). Built 1959-1963. Beautiful city views from top. Especially scenic during monsoon. Morning early visit avoids crowds. Big fair during Shravan month.

Jagannath Temple

Replica of Puri Jagannath Temple. Rath Yatra in June-July grand celebration. Beautiful architecture with 3 chariots. Open 6 AM - 9 PM. Daily aarti timings. Peaceful prayer atmosphere. Temple complex well-maintained with garden.

Sun Temple (Bundu)

18 wheeled chariot temple inspired by Konark. Opened 2017. Beautiful architecture with intricate carvings. Located 40 km from city. Combined with full day tour. Photography perfect. Best visited during sunset hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the sightseeing tour package?

Includes AC taxi, driver, fuel, parking charges for 8 hours/80 km. Entry tickets to monuments/gardens not included (₹20-50 per place). Guide service available at extra ₹500.

What is the best time for Ranchi sightseeing?

October to March best for pleasant weather. Start early morning (8-9 AM) to cover all places comfortably. Summer (April-June) hot but waterfalls good. Monsoon (July-Sept) excellent for greenery and waterfalls.

Can we customize the tour itinerary?

Yes, fully customizable. Choose which places you want to visit. Can skip places or add new ones. Driver familiar with all routes. Just inform your preferences while booking.

Do you provide pickup from hotel/home?

Yes, pickup from anywhere in Ranchi - hotel, home, airport, station. 8-hour package starts from pickup time. Drop back at same location or different address - your choice.
