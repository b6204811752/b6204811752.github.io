Places to Visit in Lohardaga

Sugga Bandh

Beautiful reservoir with scenic water views. Popular picnic spot for families. Boating facilities available during peak season. Peaceful atmosphere surrounded by hills and greenery.

Budha Pahad

Historical hilltop with ancient Buddhist connections. Trekking route with panoramic valley views. Archaeological significance with old ruins. Ideal spot for sunrise and sunset photography.

Lodhma Falls

Lesser-known but beautiful waterfall near Lohardaga. Best visited during and after monsoon season. Natural pool at the base for refreshing dips. Surrounded by forest creating serene atmosphere.

Lohardaga Fort Ruins

Historical fort with remnants of ancient architecture. Reflects the rich history of the region. Panoramic views of surrounding landscape. Educational value for history enthusiasts.

Kuru Village

Traditional tribal village showcasing local lifestyle. Experience authentic Oraon culture. Handicrafts and local products available. Peaceful rural atmosphere away from city rush.

Netarhat Road Viewpoints

Scenic viewpoints on the road to Netarhat hill station. Breathtaking valley and hill views. Perfect photography spots during journey. Popular stopover for travelers heading to Netarhat.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Lohardaga?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Lohardaga starts at ₹1,440 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹1,680, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹2,160. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Lohardaga from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 3 hours to reach Lohardaga from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 120 km via Ratu and NH75 route. Time may vary based on traffic conditions.

Can you take me to Sugga Bandh?

Yes, we provide packages covering Lohardaga and Sugga Bandh reservoir. The dam offers scenic views, peaceful atmosphere, and picnic spots. Best visited during post-monsoon. Full-day packages available.

Can you take me to Netarhat via Lohardaga?

Yes, Lohardaga is on the way to Netarhat hill station. We provide combined packages: Ranchi ? Lohardaga ? Netarhat. Total distance to Netarhat is 150 km (30 km from Lohardaga). Ideal for breaking journey with sightseeing.

Can I book a round trip to Lohardaga?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. You can book for same-day return covering Lohardaga town, Sugga Bandh, and Budha Pahad. Weekend getaway packages available.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting charges may apply for extended stopovers beyond 1 hour.
