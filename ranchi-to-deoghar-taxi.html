Places to Visit in Deoghar

Baidyanath Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Most sacred pilgrimage site attracting millions of devotees during Shravan month.

Trikut Hills

Beautiful hills with India's highest ropeway. Panoramic views and temples at the summit. Perfect for family picnics.

Naulakha Mandir

Stunning white marble temple costing 9 lakhs during construction. Beautiful architecture inspired by Belur Math.

Tapovan Hills

Serene location with caves and temples. Believed to be the meditation spot of Maharishi Valmiki. Natural springs and peaceful environment.

Basukinath Temple

Ancient Shiva temple 42 km from Deoghar. Completes the Baidyanath pilgrimage circuit. Peaceful surroundings and natural beauty.

Nandan Pahar

Small hillock with amusement park, boating, and ropeway. Ideal for children and family outings. Beautiful sunset views.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Deoghar?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Deoghar starts at ₹3,000 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹3,500, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹4,500. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Deoghar from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 5-5.5 hours to reach Deoghar from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 250 km via NH33, Bokaro, Dhanbad, and Jasidih route. Time may vary depending on traffic and road conditions.

Is taxi service available for Baidyanath Dham from Ranchi?

Yes, we provide 24/7 taxi service from Ranchi to Baidyanath Dham (Deoghar). Special packages available for pilgrimage tours including visits to Baidyanath Temple, Trikut Hills, and Basukinath Temple. Early morning pickups available for Shravan month devotees.

Can I book a round trip to Deoghar?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. You can book for same-day return, overnight stay, or multi-day packages. Contact us on WhatsApp for customized pilgrimage tour packages.

Do you provide taxi during Shravan month?

Yes, we provide dedicated taxi service during Shravan month for Kanwar Yatra. Advanced booking recommended as this is peak season. Special packages available for groups and families.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting charges may apply for extended stopovers beyond 1 hour.
