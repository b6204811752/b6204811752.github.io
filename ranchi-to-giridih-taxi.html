Places to Visit in Giridih

Usri Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand (12 meters). Best during monsoon season. Natural swimming pools and picnic spots. Popular for photography and nature walks.

Parasnath Hills

Highest mountain peak in Jharkhand (1,365 meters). Sacred Jain pilgrimage site with 20 temples. Trekking trails with panoramic views. Best visited during winter.

Madhuban (Parasnath)

Main Jain temple complex at the base of Parasnath Hills. Beautiful architecture with religious and historical significance. Facilities for pilgrims available.

Khandoli Dam

Scenic dam with boating facilities. Beautiful valley views and sunset points. Ideal picnic spot for families. Wildlife spotting opportunities.

Langta Baba Mandir

Famous Shiva temple with religious significance. Visited by devotees throughout the year. Peaceful environment with spiritual atmosphere.

Coal Belt Tourism

Explore the coal mining heritage of Giridih district. Learn about coal industry history. Unique industrial tourism experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Giridih?

How long does it take to reach Giridih from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 3.5 hours to reach Giridih from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 160 km via NH33 through Hazaribagh. Time may vary depending on traffic and road conditions.

Can you take me to Usri Falls and Parasnath Hills?

Yes, we provide taxi service to Usri Falls and Parasnath Hills (Madhuban). Full-day sightseeing packages available covering both locations. Early morning pickups available for Jain pilgrimage to Parasnath.

Can I book a round trip to Giridih?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. You can book for same-day return or overnight stay for Parasnath pilgrimage. Multi-day packages available for comprehensive sightseeing.

When is the best time to visit Usri Falls?

The best time to visit Usri Falls is during and immediately after monsoon season (July to October) when the waterfall is at its full flow. We recommend booking early morning trips to avoid crowds and enjoy the natural beauty.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting charges may apply for extended stopovers beyond 1 hour.
