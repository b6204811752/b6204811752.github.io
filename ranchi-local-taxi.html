Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in half day and full day packages?

Half day: 4 hours + 40 km for ₹1,000. Full day: 8 hours + 80 km for ₹1,800. Includes driver, fuel, parking. Extra time ₹200/hr, extra km ₹12/km. Driver meal allowance included.

Can I extend the booking if I need more time?

Yes, easily extendable. Extra hours charged at ₹200/hr. Inform driver in advance if possible. No problem extending half day to full day - just pay the difference.

What areas are covered in Ranchi local service?

All Ranchi city and nearby areas within 40-50 km: Main Road, Lalpur, Doranda, Kanke, HEC, Hinoo, Namkum, Bariatu, Ormanjhi, Khunti Road areas. For farther distances, outstation rates apply.

Do you provide monthly local taxi packages?

Yes, monthly packages for regular users: office commute, business meetings, medical appointments. Customized pricing based on daily hours and km. Great savings for regular users. Contact us for quotation.

Can I book for early morning or late night?

Yes, 24/7 service available. Small night charge (₹50 extra) for bookings starting between 11 PM - 5 AM. Popular for early morning airport drops, hospital emergencies.

What if I exceed the km limit in the package?

Extra km charged at ₹12/km beyond package limit. Driver tracks with meter. No surprise charges - you'll know the distance covered. Most city tours fit well within package limits.
