Railway Stations We Serve

Ranchi Junction (RNC)

Main railway station in city center. Major trains from Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Patna. Multiple platforms. Pickup from main entrance or platform gate. Share PNR for tracking arrival. Driver coordinates 30 minutes before arrival.

Hatia Station (HTE)

Terminus for many long-distance trains. 12 km from city center. Important for Rajdhani, Duronto trains. Less crowded than Junction. Easy pickup from station exit. Good connectivity to all city areas.

Station to City Transfers

Popular destinations: Main Road (₹200), Lalpur (₹250), Doranda (₹200), Kanke (₹300), HEC (₹350), Hinoo (₹250). All areas of Ranchi covered with fixed fares.

Station to Airport Connection

Ranchi Junction to Airport: 30 km, ₹500, 45 minutes. Hatia to Airport: 25 km, ₹400, 40 minutes. Perfect for connecting flights and trains. Time-coordinated pickups.

Direct Outstation Departures

Skip home - go directly from station to outstation destinations. Popular: Jamshedpur, Bokaro, Dhanbad, Deoghar. Save time on arrival, start journey immediately. Luggage loaded from platform.

How to Book

Share train number, arrival time, date via WhatsApp. Driver assigned with contact number. Driver tracks live train status. Waits at designated pickup point. No waiting charges for train delays up to 2 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a railway station taxi in Ranchi?

WhatsApp us at +91-7488341848 with your train number, arrival time, and destination. Driver will be assigned with contact details. Can book 1-7 days in advance or on-demand.

What if my train is delayed?

Our drivers track live train status. No extra waiting charges for delays up to 2 hours. Driver will coordinate with you and adjust pickup time automatically.

Where will the driver meet me?

Driver will coordinate via call/WhatsApp. Usually at main station exit or specific platform gate. You'll receive driver photo, name, car number before arrival. Easy coordination.

Do you provide service for early morning/late night trains?

Yes, 24/7 service available. Early morning 3-5 AM pickups and late night 11 PM-2 AM arrivals - all covered. Minimal night charges (₹50 extra for 11 PM-5 AM).

Can I book taxi from station directly to outstation?

Yes, direct outstation cabs available from station pickup. Popular: Jamshedpur (₹1,500), Bokaro (₹1,080), Deoghar (₹2,040). Skip home, go directly to your destination with luggage.

Is luggage assistance provided?

Yes, driver assists with loading/unloading luggage. Ample boot space in all vehicles for suitcases and bags. Innova recommended for families with heavy luggage (6-7 bags capacity).
