Places to Visit in Gumla

Nag Falls

Stunning waterfall with unique serpentine shape. Located 25 km from Gumla. Best during monsoon season. Natural pools and scenic surroundings perfect for picnics and photography.

Hapamuni (Sacred Spring)

Natural spring with religious significance. Crystal clear water throughout the year. Believed to have medicinal properties. Peaceful temple complex surrounded by greenery.

Nagfeni Temple

Ancient temple near Nag Falls. Religious importance for locals. Beautiful architecture amidst natural beauty. Attracts devotees during festivals and special occasions.

Palkot Hill

Scenic hill with panoramic valley views. Historical fort ruins on summit. Trekking trails for adventure enthusiasts. Ideal spot for nature photography and hiking.

Saitoli Forest

Dense forest area with rich flora and fauna. Wildlife spotting opportunities. Nature trails and bird watching. Perfect for eco-tourism and peaceful nature walks.

Oraon Tribal Villages

Experience Oraon tribal culture and traditions. Traditional dance and music performances. Handicrafts and local cuisine. Learn about indigenous lifestyle and customs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Gumla?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Gumla starts at ₹1,080 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹1,260, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹1,620. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Gumla from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 2.5 hours to reach Gumla from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 90 km via NH75 and Sisai route. Time may vary depending on traffic conditions.

Can you take me to Nag Falls and Hapamuni?

Yes, we provide packages covering Gumla, Nag Falls (25 km from Gumla), Hapamuni Temple with sacred spring, and other tourist spots. Full-day sightseeing available. Nag Falls is best visited during monsoon.

Can I book a round trip to Gumla?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. You can book for same-day return covering Gumla town and Nag Falls. Perfect for weekend trips from Ranchi.

When is the best time to visit Nag Falls?

The best time to visit Nag Falls is during and after monsoon season (July to October) when the waterfall is at its maximum flow. However, the falls have water throughout the year. Early morning visits recommended for better photography.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting charges may apply for extended stopovers beyond 1 hour.
