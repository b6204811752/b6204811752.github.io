About Jonha Falls (Gautamdhara)

Jonha Waterfall (140 ft)

Beautiful 140-foot waterfall on Raru River (Gupta Ganga). Also known as Gautamdhara Falls. Multi-tiered cascade creating misty atmosphere. Best during monsoon and post-monsoon for maximum water flow.

Buddhist Temple

Sacred Buddhist pilgrimage site at waterfall base. Legend says Lord Buddha bathed here. Small temple and meditation area for devotees. Peaceful spiritual atmosphere amidst nature's beauty.

Shiva Temple

Ancient Shiva temple near waterfall. Daily prayers and rituals performed. Attracts both Buddhist and Hindu pilgrims. Special significance during Mahashivratri and Buddha Purnima festivals.

Descending Steps (750+)

Over 750 steps leading down to waterfall base. Moderate difficulty, suitable for most visitors. Scenic views during descent through forest. Takes 20-30 minutes to reach bottom, longer to climb back.

Viewing Platforms

Multiple viewpoints at different heights. Top viewpoint offers panoramic waterfall view. Lower platforms for close-up photography. Rainbow formations visible during sunny afternoons in mist spray.

Forest & Nature Trails

Dense sal forest surrounding the waterfall. Rich biodiversity with various bird species. Nature trails for eco-tourism enthusiasts. Cool shaded areas perfect for relaxation and picnics.

How long does it take to reach Jonha Falls from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 50 minutes to 1 hour to reach Jonha Falls from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 40 km via Kanke Road. Quick and scenic journey perfect for half-day trips.

What is special about Jonha Falls?

Jonha Falls, also known as Gautamdhara, is a sacred Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha is believed to have bathed. The 140-foot waterfall offers both spiritual significance and natural beauty, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike.

Can I book a round trip to Jonha Falls?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. Perfect for half-day trips. Driver waits at parking while you explore (2-3 hours typical visit including 750+ steps descent). Same-day return guaranteed.

How many steps are there to reach the waterfall?

There are over 750 steps descending to Jonha Falls base. The descent takes 20-30 minutes, while climbing back takes 30-40 minutes. Moderate difficulty suitable for most visitors. Elderly guests can enjoy top viewpoints without descending.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting time included for standard 2-3 hour waterfall visit.
