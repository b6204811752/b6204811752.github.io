Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Ranchi Airport from city center?

Birsa Munda International Airport (IXR) is approximately 7 kilometers from Ranchi city center. It takes 20-25 minutes by taxi depending on traffic conditions.

What is the Ranchi Airport taxi fare?

Airport taxi fare ranges from ?250-300 for Swift Dzire, ?350-400 for Innova Crysta, and ?500 for luxury sedans. All fares include fuel, waiting time, and tolls.

Do you provide taxi service for 6 AM flights?

Yes, we provide 24/7 airport taxi service. For early morning flights, we ensure pickup 2-2.5 hours before departure time. Book one day in advance for hassle-free service.

How will I find my driver at the airport?

For airport pickups, our driver will wait at the arrival gate with a name board displaying your name. We also share driver details (name, photo, car number) on WhatsApp before your arrival.

Is waiting time included in the fare?

Yes, 30 minutes of free waiting time is included in the fare for all airport pickups. This covers flight delays and baggage collection time. Extra waiting charges apply after 30 minutes.

Can I book airport taxi for outstation from Ranchi?

Yes, we provide direct outstation taxi from Ranchi Airport to cities like Jamshedpur (?1,560), Bokaro (?1,320), Dhanbad (?2,100), Patna (?3,960), and Kolkata (?5,040).
