Places to Visit in Digha

Digha Beach (Old Digha)

Main attraction - 7 km long sandy beach on Bay of Bengal. Shallow waters safe for swimming. Morning walks, sunrise viewing (5:30-6 AM beautiful). Beach shacks, local snacks. Camel rides, horse rides. ATV bikes available. Crowded but lively atmosphere. Evening sunset spectacular.

New Digha Beach

Less crowded, cleaner alternative 3 km from Old Digha. Wider beach, better facilities. Amarabati Park adjacent. Marine Drive Road scenic. Recommended for families seeking quieter beach experience. Good hotels and resorts nearby. Better parking facilities.

Marine Aquarium & Research Centre

Largest aquarium in Eastern India. Collection of marine and freshwater species - sharks, turtles, colorful fish. Educational exhibits about Bay of Bengal ecosystem. Touch pool for children. Open 10 AM - 8 PM. Entry fee nominal. Great for families with kids. Photography allowed.

Amarabati Park

Beautiful waterfront park along Marine Drive. Well-maintained gardens, walkways. Toy train for children. Musical fountain (evening 7-8 PM). Boating facilities. Food stalls. Ideal for evening relaxation after beach. Good for family picnics. Clean and peaceful environment.

Shopping & Seafood

New Digha Market - local handicrafts, shell crafts, souvenirs. Beachside hawkers sell conch shells, jewelry. Fresh seafood markets - prawns, pomfret, crabs. Numerous seafood restaurants - Bengali-style fish curry, prawn malai curry. Street food - jhalmuri, fish fry. Budget to luxury dining options.

Best Time to Visit

October to February ideal - pleasant weather (15-25°C). Avoid monsoon (June-September) - rough seas, rain. Summer (March-May) hot (30-40°C) but sea breeze helps. Weekends and holidays very crowded. Durga Puja (Oct) and New Year peak season - book advance. Off-season weekdays best for budget travelers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Digha?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Digha starts at ₹7,800 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹9,100, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹11,700. Weekend beach packages with round trip available.

How long does it take to reach Digha from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 13-14 hours to reach Digha from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 650 km via Purulia and Kharagpur route. Overnight journey with rest stops recommended for comfortable trip.

What is special about Digha?

Digha is West Bengal's most popular and affordable sea beach resort on Bay of Bengal. Famous for 7 km long sandy beach, spectacular sunrise/sunset, fresh seafood, marine aquarium, family-friendly atmosphere, and budget beach vacation options.

Do you provide weekend beach packages?

Yes, we offer 2-day/1-night and 3-day/2-night weekend beach packages with round trip. Package includes drop at hotel, local sightseeing in Digha, and return to Ranchi. Can combine with nearby Mandarmani and Shankarpur beaches.

When is the best time to visit Digha?

Best time: October to February for pleasant weather (15-25°C) and calm seas. Avoid monsoon (June-September) due to rough seas and rain. Peak season during Durga Puja and winter holidays - book 2-3 months advance. Weekdays less crowded than weekends.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC on throughout journey. Driver will stay in Digha during your vacation (accommodation arranged if multi-day stay).
