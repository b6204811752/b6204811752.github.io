About Hundru Falls

Main Waterfall (320 ft)

Jharkhand's highest waterfall at 320 feet (98 meters). Subernarekha River creates spectacular cascade. Best during monsoon with maximum water flow. Multiple viewpoints for photography and nature appreciation.

Trekking Trails

Well-marked trails leading to waterfall base and viewpoints. Moderate difficulty suitable for families. Steps and railings for safety. Scenic forest walk with birdwatching opportunities along the way.

Natural Pool

Safe swimming area at waterfall base during peak season. Cool crystal-clear water perfect for refreshing dips. Best visited in groups with safety precautions. Lifeguard services during tourist season.

Photography Points

Multiple scenic viewpoints for waterfall photography. Capture rainbow formations during sunny afternoons. Panoramic valley views from hilltop. Best lighting: morning 8-10 AM and evening 4-6 PM.

Picnic Spots

Designated picnic areas with seating arrangements. Shaded spots under trees near waterfall. Food stalls selling local snacks and beverages. Clean facilities and waste management systems available.

Combined Waterfall Tour

Combine Hundru with nearby Jonha Falls (35 km). Full-day package covering both waterfalls. Popular for weekend getaways from Ranchi. Transportation between both sites included in tour packages.
