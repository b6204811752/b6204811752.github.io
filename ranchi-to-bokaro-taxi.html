About the Journey

The Ranchi to Bokaro route covers 110 kilometers via NH23, one of the best-maintained highways in Jharkhand. The journey takes approximately 2.5 hours with minimal stops. The route passes through Ramgarh (40 km from Ranchi) and Chandankiyari before reaching Bokaro Steel City. The highway is well-lit with good food outlets at Ramgarh.

Our experienced drivers are familiar with this route and ensure a comfortable journey. The fare includes all toll charges and parking fees. This is a popular route for both business and leisure travelers visiting Bokaro Steel Plant or for shopping and entertainment in Bokaro.

Top Places to Visit in Bokaro

Make the most of your trip to Steel City

1

Bokaro Steel Plant

One of India's largest steel plants, operated by SAIL. Industrial tours available for groups. A must-visit for engineering enthusiasts to see steel-making process.

2

City Park

Beautiful urban park in the heart of Bokaro with gardens, walking tracks, and children's play area. Perfect for evening walks and family picnics.

3

Jawaharlal Nehru Biological Park

Popular zoo with variety of animals and birds. Well-maintained park with lush greenery. Great educational experience for children and families.

4

Parasnath Hills

Major Jain pilgrimage site near Bokaro. Beautiful trekking destination with panoramic views. 27 Jain temples at various points on the hill.

5

Ram Mandir

Beautiful Hindu temple complex dedicated to Lord Rama. Peaceful atmosphere with well-maintained gardens. Popular for religious and cultural events.

6

City Centre Mall

Modern shopping and entertainment complex. Features multiplex cinema, food court, retail stores, and gaming zones. Great for shopping and family entertainment.
