Route Information: Ranchi to Patna

Distance & Time

330 km via NH33 through Hazaribagh, taking approximately 7 hours with normal traffic

Best Route

Ranchi → Ramgarh → Hazaribagh → Koderma → Nawada → Patna. Smooth highway drive.

Food Stops

Hazaribagh and Koderma have good restaurants. Driver knows best dhaba stops.

Petrol Pumps

Multiple fuel stations on NH33. Indian Oil, HP, Bharat Petroleum available throughout.

Best Travel Time

Start early morning (5-6 AM) to reach Patna by afternoon. Avoid night driving.

Toll Charges

Approximately ₹400-500 in toll charges (not included in fare). Paid at toll plazas.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the taxi fare from Ranchi to Patna?

Ranchi to Patna taxi fare starts at ₹3,500 for sedan (Swift Dzire/Etios) and ₹5,500 for Innova Crysta. Round trip costs ₹7,000 for sedan. Toll charges (₹400-500) are extra.

How long does it take to reach Patna from Ranchi by taxi?

The journey takes approximately 7 hours covering 330 km via NH33. Time may vary based on traffic, weather, and stops. Our drivers maintain safe speeds throughout.

Can I book one-way taxi from Ranchi to Patna?

Yes, we offer one-way taxi from Ranchi to Patna. Unlike Ola/Uber, we don't charge round trip for one-way. You pay only for the distance you travel.

Is night travel safe on Ranchi-Patna route?

We recommend day travel (5 AM - 6 PM). Night travel is possible but riskier. If needed, our experienced drivers handle night journeys safely. Extra charges may apply.

What are the pickup points in Ranchi?

We pickup from anywhere in Ranchi - your home, hotel, Ranchi Airport, Ranchi Railway Station, office, or any address you specify. Door-to-door service included.

Can I stop at places during the journey?

Yes, you can stop for meals, washroom breaks, and sightseeing. Popular stops include Hazaribagh, Koderma. Reasonable stops are free - extended halts charged extra.
