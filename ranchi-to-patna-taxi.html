About the Journey

The Ranchi to Patna route covers 330 kilometers via NH33 and NH2, one of the most important highways connecting Jharkhand and Bihar. The journey takes approximately 7 hours with stops. The route passes through scenic locations like Ramgarh and Hazaribagh before entering Bihar. Major stops include Koderma and Gaya, where you can take breaks for refreshments. The highway is well-maintained with multiple food outlets and fuel stations.

Our experienced drivers are familiar with this route and ensure a comfortable journey. The fare includes all toll charges and parking fees. We recommend starting early morning to reach Patna by afternoon.

Top Places to Visit in Patna

Make the most of your trip

1

Golghar

Historic granary built in 1786 by Captain John Garstin. Climb 145 spiral steps for breathtaking panoramic views from the top. A must-visit architectural marvel.

2

Patna Museum

One of India's oldest museums with archaeological and historical artifacts including Mauryan sculptures and Buddhist relics.

3

Gandhi Maidan

A historic open ground where major political rallies and cultural events take place. Perfect for evening walks and family picnics.

4

Takht Sri Patna Sahib

One of the holiest Sikh shrines, birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh Ji. Beautiful white marble architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

5

Mahavir Mandir

One of the most visited Hindu temples in Patna, dedicated to Lord Hanuman. Known for granting wishes to devotees.

6

Patna Planetarium

Asia's largest planetarium with astronomy shows and exhibitions. Great educational experience for families and students.
