Places to Visit in Purulia

Ayodhya Hills

Beautiful hill range with sunrise viewpoints. Trekking trails, tribal villages, and paragliding opportunities. Best visited during winter months.

Joychandi Pahar

Popular trekking destination with temple at summit. Panoramic views of surrounding landscape. Perfect for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Panchet Dam

Large reservoir with boating facilities. Beautiful sunset views and picnic spots. One of the largest earthen dams in India.

Chhau Dance Village

Famous for traditional Chhau mask dance. UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage. Watch live performances and buy authentic masks.

Bamni Falls

Scenic waterfall 12 km from Purulia. Best during monsoon season. Trekking and photography paradise with natural pools.

Pakhi Pahar (Bird Hill)

Bird watching paradise with migratory birds. Beautiful landscape with rock formations. Ideal for nature photography and peaceful walks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Purulia?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Purulia starts at ₹1,800 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹2,100, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹2,700. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Purulia from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 3.5 hours to reach Purulia from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 150 km via NH33. Time may vary depending on traffic and road conditions.

Can you take me to Ayodhya Hills from Ranchi?

Yes, we provide taxi service to Ayodhya Hills via Purulia. Full-day packages available covering Ayodhya Hills, Joychandi Pahar, Bamni Falls, and Panchet Dam. Our drivers are familiar with all hill routes.

Can I book a round trip to Purulia?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. You can book for same-day return or overnight stay. Multi-day packages available for exploring all hill stations and waterfalls in the region.

Is the route safe for night travel?

Yes, the Ranchi-Purulia route via NH33 is generally safe for night travel. However, we recommend daytime travel to enjoy the scenic beauty. For night bookings, we provide experienced drivers familiar with the route.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting charges may apply for extended stopovers beyond 1 hour.
