Places to Visit in Chaibasa

Rank Falls

One of the most beautiful waterfalls in Jharkhand. Located 40 km from Chaibasa. Best during monsoon season. Natural pools perfect for swimming. Surrounded by dense forests.

Joba Hill

Scenic hilltop with panoramic views of Chaibasa town. Trekking trails available. Popular spot for sunrise and sunset. Rock formations and natural beauty abound.

Ho Tribal Villages

Experience authentic Ho tribal culture and traditions. Traditional dance, music, and handicrafts. Learn about indigenous lifestyle. Warm hospitality and unique cultural practices.

Kali Mandir

Famous temple dedicated to Goddess Kali. Religious significance for locals. Visited during Navratri and other festivals. Beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

Icha Falls

Lesser-known but equally beautiful waterfall. Serene location with minimal crowds. Perfect for picnics and nature photography. Best visited during and after monsoon.

Saranda Forest

Dense sal forest known for wildlife. One of Asia's largest sal forests. Elephant corridors and rich biodiversity. Eco-tourism opportunities available.
