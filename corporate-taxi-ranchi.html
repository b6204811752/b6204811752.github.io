Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide GST invoices?

Yes, proper GST invoices provided for all corporate bookings. Invoice includes company name, GSTIN, trip details, HSN code. Monthly consolidated invoices also available for regular clients.

What are the payment terms for corporate accounts?

Flexible payment terms available - Net 15 or Net 30 days after invoice. Can be monthly billing cycle. Bank transfer, cheque, or online payment accepted. Credit limit assigned based on usage volume.

How do we book taxis for employees?

Dedicated WhatsApp/phone number for your company. HR/Admin can book on behalf of employees. Can share employee name and phone number - driver coordinates directly. Real-time trip tracking available.

What vehicles are available for corporate use?

Swift Dzire (4-seater) for executives, Innova Crysta (6-7 seater) for teams/families, Tempo Traveller (12-17 seater) for group transport. All vehicles AC, well-maintained, professional drivers.

Can we get monthly dedicated vehicles?

Yes, dedicated vehicles available on monthly contract. Fixed monthly cost based on estimated hours/km. Same driver assigned for familiarity. Vehicle parked at your office if needed. Very cost-effective for regular usage.

Do you serve companies outside Ranchi?

Yes, outstation corporate travel to Jamshedpur, Bokaro, Dhanbad and other cities. Regular routes for companies with frequent inter-city needs. Comfortable long-distance travel with experienced drivers.
