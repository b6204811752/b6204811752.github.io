Wedding Services We Offer

Bride Car (Dulhan Ki Gadi)

Most important wedding car - beautifully decorated for bride's grand entry. Usually Innova Crysta or Fortuner chosen for spaciousness. Flower decoration in white, pink, red - fresh roses. Driver coordinates timing with photographer. Smooth driving for lehnga comfort. Pickup from home, temple visit, arrival at venue - all covered.

Groom Car (Dulha Ki Gadi)

Groom arrival car for baarat entry. Swift or Innova popular. Decoration matching baarat theme. Pickup from getting-ready location. Driver familiar with baarat entry protocols. Waits during baarat dance and entry ceremony. Then available for other family use during wedding.

Guest Transport (Multiple Cars)

Transport for bride/groom family and VIP guests. 3-5-10 cars booked together at discounted rates. Non-decorated or simple decoration available. Pickup from hotels/homes to venue. Multiple trips if needed. Coordinates with wedding coordinator for smooth logistics.

Baarat Vehicles

Tempo Traveller (12-17 seater) for baarat party group transport. Cost-effective for moving 10-15 people together. Can follow groom's car in baarat procession. Returns baarat to hotel/home after pheras. Music system available for DJ songs during travel.

Outstation Wedding

Wedding in Jamshedpur, Bokaro, Kolkata - outstation wedding car service. Decorated car travels with family. Driver stays throughout function. Return journey next day or same night per plan. Trusted drivers for overnight stays in other cities.

Pre-Wedding Events

Cars for Haldi, Mehendi, Sangeet functions. Simple decoration sufficient for these events. Hourly or event-based packages. Same car can be used for multiple pre-wedding functions at discounted rates if booked together with wedding day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance booking is required?

Book 15-30 days in advance for wedding season (Nov-Feb, April-May). Last-minute bookings accepted if cars available. Peak wedding dates like Sundays in season book fast. Advance booking gets better car selection.

What type of decoration is included?

Fresh flower decoration - roses, marigold on bonnet, doors, back. Ribbons on sides. Small balloons. Can customize color scheme - red-white, pink-white, orange (matching your theme). Extra premium decoration at ₹1,000-1,500 extra.

When is the car decorated?

Car decorated on wedding morning - fresh flowers stay beautiful. Driver arrives 30 minutes before pickup time with decorated car. Decoration done professionally at our facility, then driven to your location.

How long is the car available?

Full day package is 8-10 hours. Usually covers morning pickup → temple/ceremony → venue → reception → drop. If wedding ceremonies extend longer, extra hours at ₹200-300/hour. Night drop after reception included.

Can we get the same car for multiple days?

Yes, book for Mehendi, Haldi, Wedding 3 different days. Discounted package rates for multi-day booking. Decoration level adjusted per event - full decoration only on wedding day, simple for other functions.

Do you provide cars for outstation weddings?

Yes, cars available for weddings in Jamshedpur, Deoghar, Kolkata etc. Outstation charges apply (₹12-14/km). Driver accommodation arranged by you or we can manage. Car decorated at destination city on wedding morning.
