About Betla National Park (Palamu Tiger Reserve)

Tiger Safari

Part of Palamu Tiger Reserve with wild tiger population. Jeep safari through dense sal forests. Early morning (6-9 AM) best for tiger sightings. Professional guides accompany safari vehicles for safety and wildlife spotting.

Wildlife Viewing

Home to elephants, leopards, sloth bears, wild boars, sambar deer, chital, and nilgai. Over 174 bird species for bird watchers. Best season: November to March (winter). Monsoon safari restricted for safety.

Palamu Fort

Historic 16th-century fort within park premises. Built by Chero dynasty kings. Architectural ruins and historical significance. Combine wildlife safari with heritage tour. Scenic viewpoint overlooking forest.

Watchtowers & Machans

Multiple elevated watchtowers for wildlife observation. Safer viewing experience from height. Perfect for photography enthusiasts. Guide-assisted viewings during safari tours. Best during evening hours (4-6 PM).

Forest Rest Houses

Government forest rest houses for overnight stays. Book in advance through forest department. Immersive jungle experience with night sounds. Multi-day safari packages recommended for better wildlife sightings.

Photography & Nature Walks

Excellent location for wildlife photography. Guided nature walks in safe zones. Flora and fauna documentation opportunities. Carry zoom lenses for close-up wildlife shots. Educational experience for nature enthusiasts.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Betla National Park?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Betla National Park starts at ₹1,800 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹2,100, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹2,700. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Betla from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 4 hours to reach Betla National Park from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 150 km via Lohardaga and Daltonganj route. Scenic journey through rural Jharkhand.

What wildlife can be seen at Betla National Park?

Betla National Park (Palamu Tiger Reserve) is home to tigers, elephants, leopards, sloth bears, wild boars, sambar deer, chital, and nilgai. Over 174 bird species including peafowl and red jungle fowl. Best wildlife viewing during winter months (November-March).

Can I book overnight safari packages?

Yes, we provide multi-day safari packages with round trip from Ranchi. Overnight stay at forest rest houses (advance booking required). 2-day/1-night package most popular for comprehensive wildlife experience. Includes multiple safari rounds.

When is the best time to visit Betla National Park?

Best time: November to March (winter) for comfortable weather and better wildlife sightings. Park remains open October to June. Monsoon season (July-September) safari restricted. Early morning (6-9 AM) and evening (4-6 PM) best for animal activity.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Safari entry fees and guide charges are separate (paid at park entrance).
