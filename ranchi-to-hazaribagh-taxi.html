Places to Visit in Hazaribagh

Hazaribagh National Park

Home to tigers, leopards, sambhar deer, and various bird species. Jeep safaris available. Best visited during winter months.

Canary Hill

Most famous sunset point in Jharkhand. Panoramic views of the town and surrounding hills. Perfect for evening picnics and photography.

Hazaribagh Lake

Beautiful urban lake with boating facilities. Surrounded by gardens and walking paths. Ideal spot for morning and evening walks.

Rajrappa Temple

Famous Shakti Peeth 60 km from Hazaribagh. Confluence of Bhairavi and Bhera rivers. Beautiful temple architecture with natural surroundings.

Konar Dam

Scenic dam on Konar River. Popular picnic spot with beautiful valley views. Migratory birds can be spotted during winter.

Isko Waterfall

Beautiful waterfall 45 km from Hazaribagh. Best visited during monsoon season. Trekking and nature photography opportunities available.

How long does it take to reach Hazaribagh from Ranchi?

Can you take me to Hazaribagh National Park?

Yes, we provide taxi service directly to Hazaribagh National Park. We can also arrange safari bookings and guide you to Canary Hill, Hazaribagh Lake, and other tourist spots. Full-day sightseeing packages available.

Can I book a round trip to Hazaribagh?

Yes, we offer round trip packages at discounted rates. You can book for same-day return or overnight stay. Full-day sightseeing packages covering Canary Hill, National Park, and Rajrappa Temple also available.

Do you provide taxi for early morning safari?

Yes, we provide early morning pickup for wildlife safari. Most safaris start at 6 AM, so we can pick you up from Ranchi around 3-3:30 AM to reach on time. Advanced booking recommended for early morning trips.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. Waiting charges may apply for extended stopovers beyond 1 hour.
