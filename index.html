How can I book a taxi in Ranchi?

What are your taxi rates for outstation trips?

Our outstation rates start from ₹9/km for hatchbacks and ₹15/km for Innova Crysta. Minimum billing is 250 km per day. Driver allowance is ₹300/day. Toll, parking, and state taxes are extra.

Do you provide airport pickup and drop services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 airport taxi services for Birsa Munda Airport. We track your flight and ensure timely pickup even if your flight is delayed. Contact us for booking.

Are your drivers experienced and verified?

Yes, all our drivers are professionally trained, verified with background checks, and have extensive driving experience. They are courteous, helpful, and know the routes well.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel free of charge up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 2 hours will incur a 20% cancellation charge.

Do you offer monthly packages for corporate clients?

Yes, we offer customized monthly packages for corporate clients with dedicated vehicles, priority support, and GST invoices. Contact us for special corporate rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and card payments. For corporate bookings, we also provide credit facilities with proper invoicing.

Which cities do you cover from Ranchi?

We cover all major cities in Jharkhand (Jamshedpur, Bokaro, Dhanbad), Bihar (Patna, Gaya, Muzaffarpur), West Bengal (Kolkata, Durgapur, Asansol), and neighboring states.
