About the Journey

The Ranchi to Dhanbad route covers 175 kilometers via NH33 and NH32, connecting two major cities of Jharkhand. The journey takes approximately 3.5-4 hours. The route passes through Ramgarh and Bokaro Steel City before reaching Dhanbad, known as the Coal Capital of India. Major stops include Bokaro (110 km from Ranchi) where you can take a break at various restaurants.

Our experienced drivers know the best route and can stop at Gomoh junction if needed. The highway is well-maintained with toll plazas and fuel stations at regular intervals. Dhanbad is an important industrial city, and this route is popular for both business and leisure travelers. All toll charges are included in the fare.

Top Places to Visit in Dhanbad

Make the most of your trip to Coal Capital of India

1

Parasnath Hills

Sacred Jain pilgrimage site with 27 temples. Highest peak in Jharkhand at 4,480 feet. Trekking and spiritual tourism destination with panoramic views.

2

Maithon Dam

Beautiful dam on Barakar River, 48 km from Dhanbad. Boat rides available. Perfect picnic spot with scenic views and gardens around the reservoir.

3

Topchanchi Lake

Scenic lake surrounded by hills and forests. Popular weekend getaway with boating facilities. Peaceful environment ideal for nature lovers and photographers.

4

Bhatinda Falls

Beautiful waterfall near Dhanbad, best during monsoon. Trekking spot with natural beauty. Great place for adventure enthusiasts and family outings.

5

Shakti Mandir

Ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Shakti. Important religious site with beautiful architecture. Peaceful atmosphere perfect for devotees and spiritual seekers.

6

ISM Dhanbad

Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), premier engineering institute with beautiful campus. Historical significance as one of India's oldest mining schools.
