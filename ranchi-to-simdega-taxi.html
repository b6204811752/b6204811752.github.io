Places to Visit in Simdega

Kamdara Dam

Beautiful dam located 15 km from Simdega. Scenic water reservoir with boating facilities. Perfect picnic spot surrounded by hills. Best visited during post-monsoon for full water levels.

Koel River

Major river flowing through Simdega district. Scenic riverside locations for relaxation. Ideal for nature photography and peaceful walks. Beautiful during monsoon season with lush greenery.

Munda Tribal Villages

Experience authentic Munda tribal culture and lifestyle. Traditional dance, music, and handicrafts. Learn about indigenous practices and customs. Tribal festivals celebrated with great enthusiasm.

Simdega Church

Historic church with beautiful architecture. Important religious site for local Christian community. Peaceful atmosphere perfect for prayer and reflection. Christmas celebrations are special events.

Forest Ranges

Dense sal forests surrounding Simdega. Rich biodiversity with various wildlife species. Nature trails and trekking opportunities. Bird watching paradise for nature enthusiasts.

Simdega Market

Local tribal market selling handicrafts and forest products. Authentic tribal jewelry, bamboo crafts, and textiles. Fresh organic vegetables and local food items. Experience local commerce and culture.

Can I book a round trip to Simdega?

Do you provide tribal cultural tour packages?

What is included in the taxi fare?
