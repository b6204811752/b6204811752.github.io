About Siliguri & Nearby Destinations

Siliguri City

Gateway to North Bengal and Northeast India. Major commercial hub at foothills of Himalayas. Hong Kong Market famous for shopping. Mahananda Wildlife Sanctuary nearby. Good hotels and restaurants for overnight stay before hill station trips.

Gateway to Darjeeling (70 km)

Siliguri is base for Darjeeling hill station. 2-3 hours drive uphill to Queen of Hills. Famous for tea gardens, toy train, Kanchenjunga views. We provide onward taxi services to Darjeeling from Siliguri.

Gateway to Sikkim (115 km)

Main entry point for Sikkim state. 3-4 hours to Gangtok via Rangpo. Permits arranged easily at Rangpo border. We offer Sikkim tour packages from Siliguri including Gangtok, Pelling, Lachung, Nathula Pass.

Kalimpong & Mirik

Kalimpong (75 km) - peaceful hill town with monasteries. Mirik Lake (50 km) - scenic lake town. Both accessible from Siliguri. Include in extended North Bengal tour packages.

Shopping & Markets

Hong Kong Market - electronics, clothes, imported goods. Bidhan Market for local products. Hill Cart Road shopping complexes. Mahabirsthan Temple for religious tourism. Good food scene with Bengali, Nepali, Tibetan cuisines.

Best Time to Visit

October to March ideal for Siliguri and onward hill tours. Avoid monsoon (June-September) for hill station routes. Summer (April-May) hot in plains but pleasant in hills. Book advance during tourist season (October-November, March-April).

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Siliguri?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Siliguri starts at ₹7,200 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹8,400, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹10,800. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Siliguri from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 12-13 hours to reach Siliguri from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 600 km via NH60 through Dumka and NH12 via Malda. Overnight journey recommended with rest stops.

Is overnight journey safe to Siliguri?

Yes, our experienced drivers are well-trained for overnight long-distance journeys. Route via Dumka and Malda has good highway conditions. We provide regular rest stops and driver rotation option for added safety.

Do you provide Darjeeling and Sikkim packages from Ranchi?

Yes, we offer complete packages: Ranchi to Darjeeling via Siliguri, Ranchi to Gangtok (Sikkim), and multi-day North Bengal tours covering Darjeeling, Gangtok, Kalimpong, Pelling. Customized itineraries available.

When is the best time to travel to Siliguri?

Best time: October to March for pleasant weather and clear Himalayan views. Peak tourist season October-November and March-April (advance booking recommended). Avoid monsoon (June-September) if planning hill station tours.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance for the long journey. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout. Driver meals and accommodation not needed as it's a same-day journey.
