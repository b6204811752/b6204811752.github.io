Places to Visit in Netarhat

Magnolia Point

Famous sunrise point offering breathtaking views. One of the best sunrise spots in Jharkhand. Early morning visits highly recommended for spectacular views of sun emerging from mountains.

Sunset Point

Spectacular sunset views over the hills. Popular evening destination with panoramic vistas. Watch the sun paint the sky in vibrant colors as it sets behind Chotanagpur plateau.

Lower Ghaghri Falls

Beautiful waterfall near Netarhat. 10 km from town center. Best during monsoon season. Trekking trails and natural pools available. Perfect for nature photography and picnics.

Upper Ghaghri Falls

Larger waterfall with more powerful flow. Serene surroundings with dense forest. Less crowded than Lower Falls. Ideal for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Netarhat Residential School

Famous boarding school established in 1954. Beautiful colonial architecture set among pine forests. Known for excellent education and scenic campus spread over 390 acres.

Pine Forests

Dense pine tree plantations creating a unique landscape. Cool climate year-round. Perfect for nature walks and photography. Refreshing atmosphere with pine fragrance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Ranchi to Netarhat?

Taxi fare from Ranchi to Netarhat starts at ₹1,800 for Swift Dzire (one way). For Innova Crysta, the fare is ₹2,100, and for luxury sedans, it's ₹2,700. Round trip packages available at discounted rates.

How long does it take to reach Netarhat from Ranchi?

It takes approximately 3.5-4 hours to reach Netarhat from Ranchi by taxi, covering a distance of 150 km via Lohardaga route. Time may vary depending on road and weather conditions in the hills.

What is the best time to visit Netarhat?

The best time to visit Netarhat is from October to March when the weather is pleasant (10-25°C). Early morning trips are recommended for sunrise views at Magnolia Point. Monsoon season (July-September) is ideal for waterfall visits.

Can I book a taxi for sunrise viewing?

Yes, we provide early morning pickups for sunrise viewing at Magnolia Point. We recommend leaving Ranchi around 2-3 AM to reach Netarhat before sunrise (around 5:30-6:00 AM). Advance booking recommended.

Are overnight stay packages available?

Yes, we offer multi-day packages including overnight stays in Netarhat. You can enjoy both sunrise and sunset, visit waterfalls, and explore pine forests. We can help with hotel recommendations in Netarhat.

What is included in the taxi fare?

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowance. No hidden charges. AC will be on throughout the journey. For overnight trips, driver accommodation is included.
